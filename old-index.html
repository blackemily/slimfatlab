A Different Way to Look at Weight Loss

Many weight-loss sites jump straight to restriction, hacks, and product pitches. SlimFatLab takes a different route.

Instead of assuming the problem starts with calories alone, this site looks at digestion, microbial balance, appetite signaling, and the way the body handles food once it gets inside you.

That does not mean calories do not matter. It means they may not tell the whole story.
